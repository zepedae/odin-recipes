Microwave Oatmeal

Description

For those of us who are too forgetful for overnight oats.

Ingredients

  • .25 cup rolled oats
  • .25 cup + splash of water
  • .25 frozen berries
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Add oats and water to microwave safe bowl
  2. Microwave at 70% power for 1:35
  3. Once oats are microwaved, immediately add peanut and stir until mixed
  4. Once peanut butter is melted and mixed add berries
