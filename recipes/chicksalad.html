Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salad with a twist.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast
  • .25 cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • .5 cup sliced green onion

Instructions

  1. Boil chicken with 1 tbsp of salt until it reaches an internal temperature of 175 degrees
  2. Combine tahini, soy sauce, sesame oil, lime juice in a large mixing bowl
  3. Add cucumber, green onion, and shredded boiled chicken to mixing bowl and stir until mixture is uniform
  4. Enjoy on sliced bread
