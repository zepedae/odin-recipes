Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salad with a twist.

Ingredients

    1.5 lbs chicken breast .25 cup tahini 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil 2 tbsp lime juice 1 tbsp salt 1 cup diced cucumber .5 cup sliced green onion

Instructions

    Boil chicken with 1 tbsp of salt until it reaches an internal temperature of 175 degrees Combine tahini, soy sauce, sesame oil, lime juice in a large mixing bowl Add cucumber, green onion, and shredded boiled chicken to mixing bowl and stir until mixture is uniform Enjoy on sliced bread
